ORGANIZATIONS

Be the “BRAVO” in TeknoKomersyo!

We, The School of Accountancy, Management, Computing, and Information Studies (SAMCIS) are dedicated to develop leaders, empower students, and create opportunities for students both in academics and extra-curricular activities. The BRAVO community welcomes you to join the organizations to help you cultivate the values of Saint Louis University

STUDENT SUPREME COUNCIL (SSC)

The KASAMA/SSC has the duty to promote and protect student interests. The KASAMA/SSC espouses student welfare and holistic development. The officers of the KASAMA/SSC do not expect fame, glory, or recognition. Rather, they expect a sense of volunteerism from the Student Community that we all help each other in making a difference in other people's lives. Not limited to our own personal spaces, not limited in our own circles, but beyond our own capacity and other people's expectations.

FOLLOW US

SCHEMA

SCHEMA serves as the official publication of SAMCIS. They are an instrument for journalistic, literacy, and artistic exposure that creates opportunities for students to hone their journalism skills. SCHEMA aims to raise awareness among the SAMCIS community by publishing articles through social media platforms or news magazines.

FOLLOW US

INTEGRATED CONFEDERACY (ICONN)

The RPG community strives to create a family, supporting and helping students to be the best. By organizing workshops that provide technical and practical insights, it creates a space to help students build confidence and self-esteem, enabling them to showcase their talent to fellow students in the fields of music, dance, sports, photography, theater ,and arts. 

FOLLOW US

GREEN CORE SOCIETY

The Green Core Society promotes environmental sustainability by providing the SAMCIS community with activities and programs that will nourish their competence in helping planet Earth by providing creative environmental solutions while inspiring others through socially involved mother nature-friendly activities and embracing our Christian-spiritedness through outreach programs.

FOLLOW US

CAMPUS MISSION VOLUNTEERS (CMV)

This organization was established for the Saint Aloysius Gonzaga Parish of SLU. It is dedicated to serving our Father made up of different ministries under the Formation, Liturgy and Social Action committees. The CMV is geared towards spreading God’s love through school programs to help those in need especially during calamities all while encouraging other Louisians to come and serve as well.

JUNIOR PHILIPPINE INSTITUTE OF ACCOUNTANTS (JPIA)

An organization guided by the four core values of SLU. It is an organization under SAMCIS that recognizes its mission to become one with the University’s mission of bringing transformative education to its students and cultivating a culture of leadership and concern that extends to the community.

FOLLOW US

MARKETING MIXERS

Marketing mixers is a nationally acclaimed student-led Marketing Association of Saint Louis University - SAMCIS and is under the affiliation of the Philippine Junior Marketing Association (PJMA), receiving recognitions from AGORA Youth Awards, PJMA, and Gawad San Luis. It is the organization’s general objective to improve and uplift Marketing education and to provide well-rounded education to marketing students socially, intellectually, and morally.

FOLLOW US

YOUNG ENTREPRENEURS’ SOCIETY

The YES Society is an organization where Louisian Entrepreneurs would have an avenue to develop and enhance their potential that is geared towards skills, talents, creativity, and innovations. It also reinforces and improves the entrepreneurial competencies that would help each individual to become a socially World-Class entrepreneur .

FOLLOW US

SICAP

The SICAP organization is an extra-curricular organization of SAMCIS that provides BLT topics through seminars that aim to help the students progress academically.

FOLLOW US

JUNIOR FINANCIAL EXECUTIVES (JFINEX)

An organization that is academically focused in promoting, uplifting, and improving the students of Financial Management through constant involvement of its members with various activities in line with Saint Louis University’s Core values along with the organization’s own core values C.H.R.I.S.T ( Commitment, Humility, Respect, Initiative, Sacrifice and Trust).

FOLLOW US

JUNIOR PEOPLE MANAGEMENT ASSOCIATION OF THE PHILIPPINES (JPMAP)

An organization guided by PMAP with the goal to develop and empower its members to grow from an academic level to a professional level as future people managers of the society.
